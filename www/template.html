User Identification Form (Log-in Page)

New users of the database must first register and open an account. Please press the button "Register" if you do not have a user name and password. A notification e-mail will be sent to you after successful registration.

If you have lost your password, please use the link "Forgot password?". A new password will be generated and you will receive a notification e-mail.

Users from organisations which are not members of the NORMAN Association will have limited access to the databases in terms of exporting and downloading full sets of data and QA/QC information. Access to single data is, however, enabled for all users.

For more information about NORMAN and how to become a member of the Association, please visit the norman-network.net site.
If you encounter any problems with log-in, please contact the NORMAN development team at norman@ei.sk.

The email address that you specify will be compared with the e-mail address in the database. If it is found, a new password will be sent to that e-mail address. If the search fails, the system will generate the following error message: "Incorrect e-mail address!".

The system administrator will manually check and authenticate your request and send you your new password as soon as possible.
